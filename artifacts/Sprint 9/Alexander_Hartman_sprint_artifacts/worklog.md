# Worklog — Poster Board Preparation & Assembly

## Story
TM17-216

## Description
Retrieved the physical tri-fold poster board from the professor and stored it securely for the team to use throughout the preparation process.

Designed the overall layout of the tri-fold board, determining the placement, sizing, and arrangement of all visual elements across the three panels. This included deciding where to position the project logo, website screenshots, and system diagrams to create a clear and visually organized presentation.

Printed all assigned images in the correct dimensions to fit their designated sections of the board. This required reviewing the layout plan and ensuring each image matched the intended space before printing to avoid waste or resizing issues.

Mounted printed images onto the board, carefully taping down both personally assigned sections and assisting teammates with their sections as needed. Attention was given to alignment and spacing to ensure a consistent and professional appearance across the full board.

Held a collaborative team meeting to review the assembled board, discuss any adjustments to the layout, and confirm the final design was ready for the ECS Open House presentation. The board was completed successfully and was presentation-ready by the end of the session.

## Result
Poster board fully assembled and finalized for the ECS Open House presentation.
